Origami Dog Face

Step 1

Start with a square sheet of paper.

Step 2

Fold the paper in half by folding top corner to the bottom corner.

Step 3

Fold the triangle in half by folding the left corner to the right corner.

Step 4

Unfold the paper.

Step 5

Make the dog ear by fold both corner of the triangle at the angle like you see in picture.

Step 6

The head and chin is pointy at this time. If you do not want it, fold the top and bottom. Make the dog face by drawing eyes and nose!

Done!

You just made your own origami dog head!
