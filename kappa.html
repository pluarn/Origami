Origami Kappa

How To Make: Kappa

Step 1

Start with a square sheet of paper and turn it making a diamond shape

Step 2

Fold the paper horizontally

Step 3

Unfold the paper

Step 4

Fold the paper vertically

Step 5

Unfold the paper

Step 6

Fold the top corner to the center horizontal line

Step 7

Unfold the paper

Step 8

Fold the top corner again this time to the line created in step 6

Step 9

Fold the bottom corner to the same line used in step 8 making sure both corners meet at the line but do not overlap

Step 10

Fold the top corner in half making sure the tip lines up with the crease made from the fold in step 8

Step 11

Fold the bottom corner back down halfway

Step 12

Flip the paper over



Step 13

Fold the right corner to the center line

Step 14

Fold the left corner to the center line

Step 15

Flip the paper over

Step 16

Fold the bottom right edge back making a diagonal edge

Step 17

Repeat step 16 this time with the bottom left edge

Step 18

Draw eyes on your Kappa and get creative with any other features you want to draw

Done!

You made your own origami kappa!
